Wellcome to my Rams and Sheeps game! I hope that you will enjoy it!! :)

The rules are simple. Enter a 4 digit number (xyzw) and try to guess what number i am thinking of (abcd). Ram means that a digit from xyzw is contained in abcd and it is on the same position. Sheep means that a digit from xyzw is contained in abcd, but not on the same position

      Congratulations you won!
